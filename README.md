## 🎯 What is ExamFusion Prep?

ExamFusion Prep is a **completely free**, bilingual quiz platform built for students preparing for India's competitive exams. No login, no paywall, no app to download — just open the site and start practicing.

Every single question — across every subject — appears in **both Hindi and English**, side by side, with a detailed explanation for the correct answer.

## ✨ Features

- 🌗 **Dark Mode** — one-tap toggle, works cleanly across every page regardless of its base theme
- 🔍 **Devanagari-aware Search** — search in Hindi *or* English, anywhere on the site
- 🔖 **Bookmarks** — star any question to revisit later, saved right on your device
- 📊 **Progress Tracking** — see how much of each chapter you've completed, with a one-tap reset
- 📱 **Mobile-First** — every table, quiz, and mind map is built to behave on a small screen
- 🈴 **Bilingual by Design** — not translated as an afterthought; Hindi and English are equal citizens on every page
