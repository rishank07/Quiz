💡 Quick Recall

Minerva Mills Case (1980) ne settle kiya — FR aur DPSP complementary hain, equal importance ke hain. Dono mein balance banana zaroori hai.

⚠ Exam Trap

DPSP enforceable nahi hain, lekin inhe ignore bhi nahi kiya ja sakta. Art. 37 kehta hai — ye "fundamental in governance" hain. Court inke against laws strike down kar sakta hai.

⚖️ Landmark Cases — FR vs DPSP Evolution
1951
Champakam Dorairajan Case
Fundamental Rights are above DPSPs. Amendments to FRs allowed to implement DPSPs.
1967
Golaknath Case
Parliament CANNOT curtail FRs to implement DPSPs. FRs are sacrosanct.
1973
Keshavananda Bharti Case (13 Judges)
Basic Structure Doctrine established. Laws violating Basic Structure are null — even if implementing DPSPs.
1980
Minerva Mills Case
DPSPs & FRs are complementary and of equal importance. Balance between the two must be maintained.
⚠ Exam Trap

Evolutionary order yaad rakho: Champakam (FR > DPSP) → Golaknath (FR protect karo) → Keshavananda (Basic Structure) → Minerva Mills (Both equal). Sequence BPSC mein seedha pucha jaata hai!
