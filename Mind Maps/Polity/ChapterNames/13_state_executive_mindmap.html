💡 Quick Recall — +89 Rule Link

State Executive = Centre ka mirror image. President → Governor · PM → CM · Attorney General → Advocate General · Parliament → State Legislature. Article numbers + 89 apply karo!

⚠ Exam Trap

Governor "At the pleasure of President" hota hai — 5-year term fixed nahi hai. President kabhi bhi hata sakta hai. CM "At the pleasure of Governor" hota hai.

🏛️ Governor — Key Facts
  • Constitutional Head of the State
  • Nominal Executive Head Art 166 Real Power → Chief Minister
  • Chancellor of all State Universities
  • 1st Female Governor → Sarojini Naidu 1st Woman Gov. Governor of Uttar Pradesh (then United Provinces), 1947
💡 Quick Recall

7th CAA, 1956 — One person can be Governor of more than one state. Aise cases mein salary President decide karta hai.

👑 Chief Minister — Key Facts
  • Real Executive Head of the State
  • Head of the Council of Ministers
  • Leader of the Majority Party in Legislative Assembly
  • Chairman of State Planning Board
💡 Quick Recall

CM = State ka PM. PM → President ko inform karta hai (Art. 78). Similarly, CM → Governor ko inform karta hai. Ye parallel structure hai Centre-State mein.

CM ka oath Governor dilwata hai, CJI ya HC CJ nahi. CM State Legislature ka member hona chahiye — MLA ya MLC ho sakta hai. Agar nahi hai toh 6 mahine mein elected hona padega.

🏢 Council of Ministers (State)
  • To aid and advise the Governor
  • Must be member of State Legislature If not, must get elected within 6 months
  • Appointed by Governor on advice of CM
  • Responsible to State Legislative Assembly
💡 Quick Recall

Ministers can participate in committee discussions but do NOT have voting rights in Parliamentary committees — same as Centre.

📊 Strength of Council of Ministers
Category Rule Amendment
Maximum Strength 15% of total Members of Legislative Assembly 91st Amendment
Minimum Strength Not less than 12 Ministers 91st Amendment
Delhi (Special) Max 10% of Assembly or 7 (whichever is less) 69th Amendment
⚠ Exam Trap

91st Amendment — Max 15% (same as Centre) lekin min. 12 bhi fix kiya. Delhi ke liye 69th Amendment special rule — max 10% or 7, whichever is less. Delhi special status wali state hai!

⚖️ Advocate General (Art. 165)
  • Highest Legal Officer of the State
  • Must be qualified to be a Judge of High Court
  • Appointed by the Governor
  • Main Role → Advise State Government on legal matters
  • Can participate in proceedings of State Legislature But CANNOT vote
  • Term → At the pleasure of the Governor
⚠ Exam Trap

Advocate General (State) = Art. 165 → qualified as HC Judge. Attorney General (Centre) = Art. 76 → qualified as SC Judge. State level mein qualification bar lower hai.

💡 Quick Recall — +89 Rule

Attorney General (Art. 76) + 89 = Advocate General (Art. 165). Dono Parliament/Legislature mein participate kar sakte hain, dono vote nahi de sakte!

⚠ Exam Trap — Most Common Confusions

1. Governor ka oath → CJ of HC (not CJI) · 2. Governor death penalty pardon → NAHI kar sakta · 3. Governor salary → CF of State (CF of India nahi) · 4. Advocate General qualification → HC Judge level (SC nahi)
