UAE's sex ratio (311 males per 100 females) is the highest — driven by a huge male migrant workforce — while Latvia's (85) is the lowest, since it has more women than men. Don't swap these two.
📚 Population Theories
TheoryKey Details
Malthusian Theory of PopulationPopulation grows geometrically while food supply grows arithmetically — leading to eventual scarcity
Optimum Theory of PopulationEdwin Cannan — there exists an "optimum" population size for a given resource base, beyond which living standards fall
Karl Marx's Population TheoryA social/economic critique — argues that overpopulation is caused by the unequal distribution of resources under capitalism, not biology
Demographic Transition TheoryW.S. Thomson & F. Notestein
⚠ Exam Trap
Karl Marx's population theory is a social/economic critique of capitalism — not a "biological" theory. Don't confuse it with Malthus's more mechanical, resource-based model.

Urban Settlement Hierarchy

  • Major City (Primate City — Mark Jefferson's concept)
  • City (larger than towns)
  • Million City (population over 1 million)
  • Conurbation (a large developed urban area)
  • World City (large metropolitan area with global influence)
  • Megacity (population over 10 million)
⚡ Quick Recall
Urban hierarchy (smallest to largest): City → Million City → Conurbation → Megacity → World City — each step up represents scale and global significance.
