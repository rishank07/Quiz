⚠ Exam Trap
Lake Assal (Djibouti) is Africa's lowest point at about 157 m below sea level — it's a below-sea-level depression, not an elevation, so remember the negative sign.

5th (9.2%)
By Area
Vinson Massif
Highest Point (4892 m)
  • Ice-covered, white continent
  • Coldest and most desolate continent
  • Highest average elevation of any continent
  • World's largest desert (by area — a cold/polar desert)
  • Global centre for scientific research and observation
🏔️ Mountains, Glaciers, Valleys & Research Centres
Mountains/GlaciersQueen Maud Mountains, Mount Erebus (volcanic peak), Lambert Glacier (world's largest glacier)
Valleys/PeninsulasTaylor Valley (Dry Valley), Palmer Peninsula (ice-free)
India's Research CentresDakshin Gangotri (1983), Maitri (1988-89), Bharati (2012)
📌 Important Facts
  • World's lowest recorded temperature — Vostok Station, Antarctica (−89.2°C)
  • Himadri — India's first permanent research centre (in the Arctic, not Antarctica)
⚠ Exam Trap
Himadri is India's first Arctic research station, while Dakshin Gangotri, Maitri, and Bharati are India's Antarctic stations — don't mix up the two polar regions.
