🌾 Plains
  • Flat region – covers 41% of the Earth's surface
📂 Types of Plains
Type of PlainExamples
Karst Plains (Limestone region)Serbia, Montenegro, and Nullarbor Plain (Australia)
Alluvial PlainsIndus-Ganga Plain, Mississippi Plain, Nile Plain, Danube Plain, Yangtze-Kiang Plain, etc.
Glacial PlainsPlains of Canada, Finland, and Sweden
Loess PlainNorthern China Plain (Wind-deposited plain)
Coastal PlainsFlorida Plain, Coromandel Coast Plain
Uniform PlainsParis Basin, Central Russian Plain
📌 Netherlands Plain Known as "Plain of Lakes"
⚡ Quick Recall
Alluvial Plains (e.g. Indus-Ganga) are formed by river sediment deposition; Loess Plains (e.g. Northern China) are formed by wind-deposited silt.
