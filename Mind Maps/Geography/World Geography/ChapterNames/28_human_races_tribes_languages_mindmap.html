🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Human Races

White Race / Caucasoid

  • European origin
  • Found in: Europe, Americas, West Asia, North Africa

Yellow / Mongoloid Race

  • Asiatic origin
  • Found in: East Asia, Central Asia, Americas (Native Americans)

Black Race / Negroid

  • African origin
  • Found in: Sub-Saharan Africa, parts of Oceania
⚡ Quick Recall
All three major races have spread beyond their origin continent through migration — e.g., Mongoloid peoples are also Native Americans, and Caucasoid peoples live across the Americas too.

⚠ Exam Trap
Bushmen (Kalahari) and Pygmies (Congo Basin) are both African tribes, but from very different regions and environments — desert vs. rainforest — a common region-matching trap.
