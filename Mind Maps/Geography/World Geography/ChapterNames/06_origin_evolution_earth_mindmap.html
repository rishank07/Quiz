  • First Hypothesis: Comte de Buffon (French Scientist) – 1749 AD
1749 AD
First Hypothesis (Comte de Buffon)
🧭 Two Concepts on Origin of Earth & Planets
  • Monistic Concept / Parental Concept: Earth and planets originated from the Sun itself
  • Dualistic Concept (Binary Concept): Earth and planets originated due to interaction between the Sun and another celestial body
⚡ Quick Recall
Monistic = one body (Sun alone) involved · Dualistic = two bodies involved

🌐 Shape / Form of the Earth
  • Shape: Geoid or Flattened Ellipsoid
  • Slightly flat at top and bottom
ParameterDetails
Reason for Geoid ShapeGreater centrifugal force (at equator) → Bulging at centre • Gravitational force at the poles → Pull surface towards the centre
Earth's Mass5.97 × 10²⁴ Kg
Aquatic Part71%
Terrestrial Part29%
⚡ Quick Recall
Earth is wider at the Equator and flatter at the poles — Geoid / Flattened (Oblate) Ellipsoid shape.
