⚡ BPSC KEY FACTS — Bihar Connection
  • Jharkhand carved from Bihar on 15 November 2000
  • After split, Bihar lost minerals + tribal areas to Jharkhand
  • Bihar's NW border = UP | NE = WB | South = Jharkhand | North = Nepal
  • Bihar has no coastline (landlocked state)
