🗂️ Classification of Lakes
🌍 Tectonic Lakes
Formed by tectonic activity — e.g. Wular Lake (J&K)
🌊 Lagoon / Backwater
Coastal saline/brackish — e.g. Chilika, Vembanad, Pulicat
🧊 Glacial Lakes
High altitude — e.g. Kedartal, Tsongmo, Roopkund, Dal
💨 Wind-formed Lakes
Erosion by wind — e.g. Sambhar, Didwana, Pachpadra (Rajasthan)
🌋 Crater Lakes
Volcanic/Meteorite — e.g. Lonar Lake (Maharashtra)
🏗️ Man-made Lakes
Dams & reservoirs — e.g. GB Pant Sagar, Govind Sagar

🌊 Tectonic Lakes
🏆 Wular Lake — J&K Ramsar 1990
  • Location: Bandipora district, J&K
  • Largest Freshwater lake in India
  • Located on river Jhelum
  • At foothills of Harmukh mountain
  • Result of tectonic activities
  • Ramsar Convention: 1990
🌊 Lagoon / Backwater Lakes
🐟 Chilika Lake — Odisha Ramsar 1981
  • Location: Puri, Khordha and Ganjam districts, Odisha
  • India's largest brackish water lagoon
  • Area: 1165 km²
  • UNESCO Tentative World Heritage Site: 2014
  • Ramsar Site: 1981 (first Indian Ramsar site)
🚣 Vembanad Lake — Kerala Ramsar
  • Location: Kottayam, Alappuzha, Ernakulam border
  • India's longest lake
  • Famous boat race held annually
  • Confluence of 10 rivers (mainly Pamba and Periyar)
  • Two islands: Vallarpadam + Wellington
  • Also called Kayal (Kerala backwater)
🦩 Pulicat Lake — AP + TN
  • Location: AP-Tamil Nadu border
  • India's 2nd largest brackish water lake
  • Mangrove forests on shore
  • Pulicat Lake Bird Sanctuary here
🔥 Sambhar Lake — Rajasthan Ramsar 1990
  • Largest inland saline lake
  • Migratory birds: Pink Flamingo in winter
  • Ramsar: 1990
  • Formed by wind erosion

🌋 Crater Lake — Lonar Lake (Maharashtra)
  • Formed by meteorite impact (NOT volcanic)
  • Caldera = expanded volcanic crater (general definition)
  • Lonar = only saline crater lake of India
💨 Wind-formed Lakes (Rajasthan)
  • Sambhar Lake — Rajasthan (largest inland saline)
  • Didwana — Rajasthan
  • Pachpadra — Balotra district, Rajasthan
  • Wind erosion lake = Playa
🚨 EXAM TRAP
  • Chilika Ramsar = 1981 (India's FIRST Ramsar site)
  • Sambhar Ramsar = 1990 | Wular Ramsar = 1990
  • Lonar = meteorite crater (NOT volcanic!) — Maharashtra
  • Vembanad = India's longest lake (NOT largest area)
