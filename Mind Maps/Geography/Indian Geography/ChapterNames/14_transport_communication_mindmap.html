📍 Bihar Special — Transport & Communication
🚂 East Central Railway — Hajipur!
  • East Central Railway HQ = Hajipur (Bihar)
  • Most important railway zone for Bihar
  • Covers: Bihar + Jharkhand regions
  • BPSC mein yeh question almost har exam mein aata hai!
🌊 NW-1 — Ganga Waterway Through Bihar
  • NW-1: Allahabad (Prayagraj) → Haldia (1,620 km)
  • Passes through Bihar via Ganga River
  • Patna → Bhagalpur → Munger = major Bihar cities on NW-1
  • Declared national waterway: 22 October 1986
  • Important for Bihar's inland water transport potential
🛣️ Bihar in Road Network
  • NH-44 (Srinagar–Kanyakumari) — India's longest NH passes through many states
  • NH-30 (Sitarganj→Ibrahimpatnam) — 5th longest, passes through Eastern India
  • PMGSY — connects villages in Bihar (500+ population)
  • Bihar has dense road network due to large population
✈️ Bihar — Air Connectivity
  • Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport = Patna
  • Other airports: Gaya, Darbhanga, Muzaffarpur
  • Gaya = Buddhist circuit tourism airport
  • Darbhanga = recently upgraded to commercial flights
🚂 North Eastern Railway — Gorakhpur (Near Bihar)
  • North Eastern Railway HQ = Gorakhpur (UP)
  • Covers border areas of UP + Bihar
  • Gorakhpur = important railway junction near Bihar border
⚡ BPSC MASTER RECALL — Transport Bihar
  • East Central Railway = Hajipur, Bihar (most important!)
  • NW-1 = Prayagraj → Haldia = passes through Bihar (Ganga)
  • Patna airport = Jay Prakash Narayan International
  • North Eastern Railway = Gorakhpur (covers Bihar border areas)
  • Bihar ka NW-1 se link = Ganga waterway = Patna, Bhagalpur
🚨 BPSC EXAM TRAP
  • East Central Railway = Hajipur (Bihar) — NOT Patna!
  • NW-1 starts from Prayagraj (Allahabad) — NOT from Varanasi
  • IWAI HQ = Noida (NOT Patna despite Ganga being NW-1)
  • Railway Board formed = 1905 | Nationalised = 1951
