📍 Bihar Special — Industries
🍬 Sugar — Bihar Pioneered India!
  • India's first sugar mill = 1903, Saran District (Bihar)
  • Bihar = major sugar producing state
  • Major centres: Motihari (Champaran), Madhubani
  • Western Bihar + Eastern UP = major sugar belt
🚂 Railway Factories — Bihar
  • Marhowrah (Saran, Bihar) — Diesel Engine Factory
  • Madhepura (Bihar) — Electric Engine Factory (2020)
  • Bihar = hub of locomotive manufacturing!
  • These 2 factories = modern railway manufacturing for India
⚡ QUICK RECALL
Diesel = Marhowrah | Electric = Madhepura | Both in Bihar!
🌿 Jute — Bihar Centres
  • Bihar jute centres: Saharsa, Darbhanga, Katihar
  • Bihar's north (Kosi region) = jute cultivation area
  • International Jute Org HQ = Dhaka (Bangladesh)
⚗️ Sindri Fertilizer — Jharkhand (Former Bihar)
  • Sindri (Jharkhand) = Asia's largest fertilizer plant
  • Indian Fertilizer Corporation = 1951
  • Sindri was in Bihar before Jharkhand was carved out (2000)
  • BPSC often asks this in Bihar industry context
⚙️ Steel — Bokaro & TISCO (Jharkhand)
  • TISCO (1907) = Jamshedpur (Sakchi) — Jharkhand (former Bihar)
  • Bokaro Steel Plant (1964) — Jharkhand, Soviet collaboration
  • Heavy Engineering Corp (HEC): Ranchi, 1958
  • Aluminium: Muri (Jharkhand) — INDALCO
  • Central Tasar Silk Research: Ranchi (Jharkhand)
⚡ BPSC MASTER RECALL — Bihar/JH Industries
  • Sugar 1st mill = Saran, Bihar (1903)
  • Railway: Marhowrah (Diesel) + Madhepura (Electric, 2020) — both Bihar
  • Jute: Saharsa, Darbhanga, Katihar — Bihar
  • TISCO = Jamshedpur | Bokaro = Soviet | HEC = Ranchi (1958)
  • Sindri = Asia's largest fertilizer plant (Jharkhand)
  • Aluminium Muri = Jharkhand | Cement Sindri = JH
🚨 BPSC EXAM TRAP
  • Madhepura Electric Engine Factory = 2020 — very recent, frequently asked
  • Sugar first = Saran (Bihar) NOT Maharashtra or UP
  • TISCO founder = Jamshetji Tata (NOT J.R.D. Tata)
  • Bokaro = 3rd Five-year Plan + Soviet | Bhilai = 2nd Five-year Plan + Soviet
  • HEC Ranchi = Heavy Engineering (NOT TISCO or Bokaro)
