🚨 EXAM TRAP
  • Southernmost point of mainland = Kanyakumari (8°4'N)
  • Southernmost point of entire India = Indira Point, Great Nicobar (6°4'N)
  • Northernmost = Indira Col, NOT Indira Point — dono "Indira" hain, confuse mat ho!

🚨 EXAM TRAP
  • Tropic of Cancer = 23½°N (23°30'N)
  • It passes through 8 states — 5 nahin, 6 nahin → sirf 8
  • IST + Tropic of Cancer intersection = Chhattisgarh ONLY (Koriya/Sonhat)
  • Aizawl (Mizoram capital) — Mizoram se Tropic of Cancer guzarti hai, lekin capital north mein hai
