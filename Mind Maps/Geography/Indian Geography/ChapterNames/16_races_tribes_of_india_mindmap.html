🎺 Toda Tribe — Tamil Nadu
  • Toda = famous tribe of Nilgiri Hills, Tamil Nadu
  • Known for buffalo pastoralism
  • Distinctive curved barrel-shaped houses
  • Kota = craftsmen tribe of Nilgiri
🚨 EXAM TRAP
  • Sentinelese = most isolated tribe in world — North Sentinel Island, A&N
  • Toda = Tamil Nadu (Nilgiri Hills) — buffalo pastoralists
  • Kurumba = found in both Kerala AND Tamil Nadu
  • Jarawa (Jarwa) = also A&N — different from Sentinelese (both avoid contact)

🏹 Santhal
  • Largest tribe of Jharkhand
  • Found in: Santhal Pargana, Jharkhand + WB + Odisha
  • Santhal Rebellion (1855-56) — against British
  • Leaders: Sidhu and Kanhu
🏹 Munda
  • Ancient tribe of Chotanagpur Plateau
  • Birsa Munda — legendary tribal leader (Ulgulan)
  • Munda Rebellion = Ulgulan (Great Tumult)
  • Found in: Ranchi, Khunti (Jharkhand)
🏹 Oraon (Kurukh)
  • Also called: Kurukh
  • Found in: Ranchi, Lohardaga, Gumla (Jharkhand)
  • Also in Bihar + WB + Odisha
  • Language: Kurukh (Dravidian origin)
🏹 Ho Tribe
  • Found in: Singhbhum district (Jharkhand)
  • Related to Munda group
  • Language: Ho (Austroasiatic)

📍 Bihar — Tribal Context
  • Bihar's ST% = relatively low (most tribes in Jharkhand — carved out 2000)
  • Before 2000: Santhal, Munda, Oraon, Ho = all in "Bihar"
  • Present Bihar = some tribal communities in border areas
  • WB border = Santhal presence in Bihar's eastern areas
  • Tharu tribe = found in Bihar's Champaran (Terai area)
⚡ BPSC MASTER RECALL — Tribes
  • Jharkhand major tribes: Santhal, Munda, Ho, Oraon, Birhor, Kharia
  • Santhal = largest in JH | Santhal Rebellion = 1855 (Sidhu-Kanhu)
  • Birsa Munda = Ulgulan (1899-1900) | Found in Ranchi-Khunti area
  • Bhil = India's largest tribe | Gond = 2nd largest
  • Mizoram = highest ST% state (94.4%) | MP = highest ST number
  • Tharu = Bihar's Champaran (Terai) + Uttarakhand
🚨 BPSC EXAM TRAP
  • Santhal Rebellion = 1855 (Hul) | Leaders = Sidhu and Kanhu (NOT Birsa)
  • Birsa Munda = Munda tribe leader | Rebellion = Ulgulan (1899-1900)
  • Oraon = also called Kurukh — language is Dravidian despite being in NE India
  • Birhor = one of India's most primitive tribes (Jharkhand)
  • Tharu = Bihar (Champaran Terai) — ye Bihar ka apna tribe hai!
