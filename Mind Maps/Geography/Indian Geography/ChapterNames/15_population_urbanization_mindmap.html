📊 Census — Basic Facts
📋 Census — Constitutional Provisions
  • Definition: Official count, every 10 years
  • Responsibility: Central Government (Article 246)
  • List: Union List
  • Key Officer: Registrar General & Census Commissioner
  • 2011 Census = India's 15th Census (7th since Independence)
  • Census began: 1872 (irregularly — Lord Mayo)
  • Regular census began: 1881 (Lord Ripon)

📚 Bihar — Literacy Lowest in India!
  • Bihar literacy = 61.80% — lowest among all states
  • National avg = 74.04% — Bihar is 12+ points below national avg
  • Female literacy especially low in rural Bihar
  • Better than: only if compared to some districts
🚨 BPSC KEY FACT
Bihar = lowest literate state (61.80%) | This is asked in almost every BPSC exam!
👥 Bihar — Density Record
  • Bihar = most densely populated state in India (1106/km²)
  • WB = 2nd (1028/km²) — very close to Bihar
  • Bihar = small state geographically but massive population
  • Northern Bihar (flood plains) = extremely high density
  • Reason: flat alluvial soil = agricultural + settlement
⚖️ Bihar — Sex Ratio
  • Bihar sex ratio = moderate (not in extreme lists)
  • National avg = 943
  • UP = 912 (low) | Kerala = 1084 (high)
  • Bihar improving but female literacy gap = challenge
🕌 Bihar — Religion & SC/ST
  • SC % in Bihar: UP = 20.7% (neighbour) | Bihar has significant SC population
  • Bihar SC major communities: Chamar, Musahar, Dusadh, Paswan
  • ST % in Bihar = relatively low (Jharkhand mein zyada)
  • Muslim population in Bihar = significant (around 16-17%)
⚡ BPSC MASTER RECALL — Bihar Population
  • Population = 10.40 crore (3rd largest)
  • Density = 1106/km² (1st highest)
  • Literacy = 61.80% (lowest state)
  • Share of India = 8.60%
  • 2011 Census 15th (7th since independence)
  • Census regular from 1881 (Lord Ripon)
  • Highest density state = Bihar | Lowest = Arunachal Pradesh
