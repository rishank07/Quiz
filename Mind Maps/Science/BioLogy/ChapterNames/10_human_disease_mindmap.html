🦠 Chapter 10 — Human Disease

General Science · Biology · BPSC / BSSC Exam Preparation
📋 Types of Diseases
Congenital
  • Genetic / developmental defects during embryonic development
  • e.g. Cerebral palsy, Haemophilia, Colour blindness
Acquired
  • Acute: Short-term (Cold, Cholera)
  • Chronic: Long-lasting (TB, Elephantiasis, Arthritis)
Infectious
  • Transmitted from sick person via contact/carrier/medium
  • Caused by pathogens: Virus, Bacteria, Protozoa, Fungi, Worms
🔀 Disease Transmission Routes
  • Free droplets in air (coughing/sneezing)
  • Contaminated food and water
  • Contaminated blood or body fluids
  • Disease vectors: Flies, Mosquitoes
  • Indirect contact: Contaminated objects
5F Rule:Flies · Fingers · Fomites · Food · Fluid
⚠️ Non-Infectious Diseases
Type Examples
Allergies Hay-fever, Asthma
Nutritional Deficiency Marasmus (protein), Goitre (iodine), Pellagra (B3), Dental cavities (fluoride)
Degenerative Diseases Heart attack, Arthritis, Diabetes, Glaucoma, Cataract
Mental Diseases Stress, Depression, Epilepsy, Anorexia nervosa
Pollution Related Arsenic (As) → Black foot disease | Cadmium (Cd) → Itai-itai | Fluoride water → Knock-Knee syndrome
Cancer Uncontrolled cell growth — Blood cancer (Leukemia), Carcinoma, Sarcoma
⚠ Exam Trap
Arsenic → Black foot | Cadmium → Itai-itai | Fluoride → Knock-Knee syndrome. Teen different pollutants, teen different diseases — confuse mat karna!
🦠 Viral Diseases — Complete Table

Chicken Pox Varicella Zoster Air borne
Measles / Rubella Rubellavirus Air borne
Influenza Orthomyxovirus Air borne
Swine Flu Influenza A Virus Subtype H1N1 Air borne
Yellow Fever Flavi Virus Bite of Aedes aegypti mosquito
Dengue (Break Bone Fever) Dengue Virus (DENV) Bite of Aedes aegypti mosquito
Chikungunya Chikungunya Virus (CHIKV) Bite of Aedes aegypti mosquito
Poliomyelitis Polio RNA virus (Type 1, 2, 3) Contaminated soil, water or food
Rabies / Hydrophobia Lyssa virus type-1 Bite of infected animal (Wolf, Dog, Monkey)

⚠ Exam Trap
Aedes aegypti mosquito = Dengue + Chikungunya + Yellow Fever (teeno same mosquito!). AIDS test = ELISA. Swine flu = H1N1. Rabies = Hydrophobia (water se darr). Polio = eradicated in India.
⚡ Quick Recall
HIV-AIDS, HPV, Hepatitis-B = Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STD). COVID-19 = Zoonotic disease (SARS-CoV-2 virus, from animals). Jaundice = symptom of hepatitis / liver inflammation.

⚡ Quick Recall

Malaria = Female Anopheles (NOT male, NOT Aedes). Kala-azar = Sand fly. African Sleeping Sickness = Tse-tse fly. Pyorrhoea = Saliva/Kiss (gum disease).

📌 Important Additional Facts
  • COVID-19 = Infectious zoonotic disease (spreads between animals and humans)
  • SARS-CoV-2 virus causes COVID-19 — originated from animal source
  • Jaundice: Skin turns yellow — symptom of liver inflammation / Hepatitis
  • Polio = Eradicated in India
  • HIV-AIDS, HPV, Hepatitis-B = Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STD)
  • Trachoma: Eye disease → blindness; caused by Chlamydia trachomatis
  • Helicobacter pylori: Causes gastritis in stomach
⚠ Exam Trap
Ringworm = Fungal disease (worm nahi hai!). Candidiasis = Candida fungus. Jaundice = Hepatitis symptom (liver). Typhoid test = Widal | AIDS test = ELISA — dono alag tests.
