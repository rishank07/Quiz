⚡ QUICK RECALL "Environment" = Environ (surrounding) + ment → literally, a covering that surrounds from all sides.
🌱 Environment — Introduction
  • Environ + ment → Environment: a covering that surrounds from all sides.

Components of Environment

  • Biotic → Living beings, animals, plants, microbes.
  • Abiotic → Water, air, soil, etc.
  • Energy → Solar energy, geothermal energy, etc.

Types of Environment

  • Natural → Land, air, water, etc.
  • Man-made → Agriculture, industrial cities, etc.
⚠ EXAM TRAP Energy (solar, geothermal) is sometimes wrongly clubbed under Abiotic — the source lists it as a distinct third component alongside Biotic and Abiotic.
⚠ Environmental Degradation
  • Environmental degradation means environmental imbalance.
  • Causes: Mining, Industrialization, Urbanization, Modern agriculture, etc.
🛡 Environmental Protection
  • Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 — led to formation of the Genetic Engineering Approval Committee.
  • National Green Tribunal (NGT), 2010 — for speedy resolution of environment-related matters.
  • Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) — key regulatory body.
⚡ QUICK RECALL Agenda-21 was adopted at the Rio Earth Summit (1992) — aimed at reducing ecological destruction and economic inequality.
