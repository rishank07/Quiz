🌳 Biosphere Reserve / Reserved Area
  • Started under UNESCO's Man and Biosphere Programme (1971).
  • Purpose: conservation of ecosystems and their genetic materials.

📌 Important Facts
  • Asian Black Bear and Snow Leopard are found in Nanda Devi and Valley of Flowers National Parks.
  • Estuarine Crocodile Conservation is carried out at Bhitarkanika Wildlife Sanctuary (Odisha).
  • First National Park of India (1936 AD) → Hailey National Park → later known as Jim Corbett National Park → renamed Ramganga National Park in 2021.
