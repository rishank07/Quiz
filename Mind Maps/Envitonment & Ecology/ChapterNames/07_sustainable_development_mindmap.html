  • Sustainable Development means minimum exploitation of natural resources while keeping future generations in mind — covering social, economic, and environmental development together.
  • The 17 SDGs were determined in 2015, became effective from 1st January 2016, with a target completion year of 2030.
⚡ QUICK RECALL Remember the sequence: SDGs Determined (2015) → Effective (1 Jan 2016) → Target (2030) — a 1-year gap between determination and implementation, and a 15-year runway to the target.
🌱 Sustainable Development — Introduction
  • Minimum exploitation of natural resources.
  • Utilization of resources keeping in mind the future (upcoming) generations.
  • Includes: Social development, Economic development, Environmental development, etc.
⚠ EXAM TRAP Sustainable Development is NOT just about environmental protection — it equally covers social and economic development. A common conceptual trap in one-liner questions.
