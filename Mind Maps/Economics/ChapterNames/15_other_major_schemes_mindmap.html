Newest Schemes (2023–24)
  • This chapter compiles major Central Government schemes frequently asked in BPSC/BSSC Prelims & Mains as one-liner / matching-type questions.
  • Focus areas: exact launch year/month, full forms of acronyms, and the core objective of each scheme.
  • Schemes are grouped thematically (Social Welfare, Financial Security, Skill Development, Agriculture & Infra, Health & Education) for logical revision — followed by a Master Table for rapid final revision.
⚡ QUICK RECALL Chronology trick — remember by "newest first": Vidyalaxmi & Bima Sakhi (2024) → Surya Ghar (Feb 2024) → PVTG Mission & PRANAM (2023) → Adi Adarsh Gram & SHRESTHA & Agnipath & SMILE (2021–22).
🆕 PM Vidyalaxmi Yojana Nov 2024
  • Provides financial assistance to students belonging to backward classes and weaker sections for higher education.
  • Aimed at reducing dropout due to financial constraints among disadvantaged student groups.
🆕 Bima Sakhi Yojana Dec 2024
  • Provides special training and financial assistance to women to help them manage a work-based (insurance-linked) programme.
  • Aimed at women's economic empowerment through skill-linked livelihood support.
🆕 PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana Feb 2024
  • Free electricity scheme for one crore families through installation of rooftop solar panels.
  • Part of India's clean/renewable energy and household savings push.
⚠ EXAM TRAP Don't confuse with PM-KUSUM (2019) — KUSUM is for farmers' solar pumps, while Surya Ghar is for household rooftop solar & free electricity.
🆕 Pradhan Mantri PVTG Vikas Mission Nov 2023
  • Aims at improvement in the social and economic status of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs).
🆕 PM PRANAM Yojana June 2023

Full Form: PM Programme for Restoration, Awareness, Nourishment and Amelioration of Mother Earth

  • Encourages balanced use of chemical fertilisers along with organic and bio-fertilisers.
  • Aims to reduce over-dependence on chemical fertiliser subsidy and promote sustainable farming.
PM Adi Adarsh Gram Yojana 2021–22
  • Focuses on all-round development of tribal villages.
SHRESTHA Yojana June 2022

Full Form: Scheme for Residential Education for Students in High Schools in Targeted Areas

  • Provides quality residential education from Class 9 to 12 to meritorious students of the SC community in targeted areas.
SMILE Feb 2022

Full Form: Support for Marginalised Individuals for Livelihood and Enterprise

  • Aims to create an inclusive society for transgenders, beggars, and other marginalised individuals so they can participate in the mainstream and live a dignified life.
One Nation One Ration Card Scheme Aug 2019
  • Enables ration card holders to get food grains at a reduced price from any government ration shop anywhere in the country.
  • Ensures portability of food security benefits, especially for migrant workers.
⚡ QUICK RECALL One Nation One Ration Card = portability of PDS benefits across states — launched August 2019.
PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana 2015
  • Provides simple term insurance of ₹2 lakh to the poor.
  • Gives financial assistance to the family in case of the insured person's death (including accidental death).
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana 22 Jan 2015
  • Encourages parents to save for the future of their daughters — covering education and marriage expenses.
  • Small savings scheme offering a high, government-backed interest rate exclusively for the girl child.
⚠ EXAM TRAP Exact launch date is frequently tested: 22 January 2015 — the same date as the "Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" campaign launch.
Agnipath Yojana 2022
  • Allows youth ("Agniveers") to serve in the armed forces for a 4-year tenure.
PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana July 2015
  • Provides skill training to youth to improve employability and productivity.
USTAAD Yojana May 2015

Full Form: Upgrading the Skills and Training in Traditional Arts / Crafts for Development

  • Recognises and upgrades the talent of artisans, sculptors, and traditional artists.
Atal Innovation Mission 2016

Also known as: Atal New Pravartana Mission

  • Promotes innovation through Atal Tinkering Labs in schools and Incubation Centres in universities.
⚡ QUICK RECALL Atal Tinkering Labs → school level | Atal Incubation Centres → university/institute level.
PM-KUSUM Yojana 2019

Full Form: PM Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthan Mahabhiyan

  • Provides financial support to farmers for setting up solar pumps.
SVAMITVA Scheme 2020

Full Form: Survey of Villages and Mapping with Improvised Technology in Village Areas

  • Provides a unified residential property ownership solution (property cards) for rural India using drone technology.
Jal Jeevan Mission 2019
  • Aims to provide clean drinking water and piped water connections (Har Ghar Jal) to all households by 2024.
PM Gatishakti Yojana 2021
  • National Master Plan to develop basic infrastructure and promote integrated, multi-modal economic development.
⚠ EXAM TRAP PM Gatishakti (infrastructure master plan, 2021) is often confused with PM PRANAM (fertiliser scheme, 2023) — different sectors entirely.
PM Poshan Shakti Nirman Yojana Sept 2021
  • New name given to the Mid-Day Meal Scheme.
  • Provides one-time cooked meal to all children studying in government and government-aided schools.
⚠ EXAM TRAP PM Poshan Shakti Nirman = renamed Mid-Day Meal Scheme (Sept 2021) — do not confuse with POSHAN Abhiyaan (2018, nutrition mission for women & children).
Safe Motherhood Assurance (SUMAN) Yojana Oct 2019
  • Aims to reduce maternal, infant, and child mortality by ensuring free and dignified quality healthcare.
UDAN Yojana Oct 2016

Full Form: Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik

  • Provides convenient and affordable air travel connectivity from/to smaller cities.
Setu Bharatam Yojana 2016
  • Aims to build bridges on national highways to ensure safe and smooth (railway-crossing-free) travel.
