Important Height Notes
  • Highest peak including Pakistan-administered Kashmir: K2 / Godwin-Austen — Pakistan-occupied Kashmir.
  • Kangchenjunga (8,586 m), on the Sikkim–Nepal border, is the highest peak in Indian-administered territory.
  • Statue of Unity (Gujarat) — tallest statue in the world; located on Sadhu Bet Island on the Narmada river.
  • Qutub Minar, Delhi — tallest minaret in India.
⚠ EXAM TRAP K2 (8,611 m) is the highest peak in territory claimed by India but lies in Pakistan-occupied Kashmir. For "highest peak actually administered by India," the correct answer is Kangchenjunga, NOT K2 — a very common trap.
