Government of India Act, 1858 — Result
East India Company ka rule khatam hua.
British Crown (Queen Victoria) supreme authority bani.
Governor-General → Viceroy ban gaya. Lord Canning = 1st Viceroy of India.

Political Causes

  • Doctrine of Lapse (Lord Dalhousie): Beta nahi → state British le lete. Satara (1848), Sambalpur (1849), Jhansi (1853).
  • Subsidiary Alliance (Lord Wellesley): Raja ko British army rakhni padti + unki marzi se chalna padta.

Economic Causes

  • Permanent Settlement: Zameen ka tax zyada → kisaan barbaad.
  • Ryotwari System: Seedhe kisaan se tax → bojh zyada.
  • Mahalwari System: Gaon ke saath tax settlement → phir bhi pareshan.

Social Causes

  • Christian missionaries bahut active — logon ko lagta tha sabko Christian bana denge.
  • Racial discrimination — British Indians se bura bartav karte the.
  • Social reforms: Sati pratha, child marriage, female infanticide pe ban → orthodox logon ko bura laga.

Immediate Cause

  • Enfield Rifle aaya — cartridge muh se kaatni padti thi.
  • Rumour: Cartridge mein Cow fat (Hindu ke liye paap) + Pig fat (Muslim ke liye haram).
  • Sipahi bahut gusse mein aaye → Spark of Revolt.
Doctrine of Lapse = Dalhousie Subsidiary Alliance = Wellesley Enfield Rifle = immediate cause Jhansi 1853 = Doctrine of Lapse

Revolt Fail Kyun Hua?
4 Major Reasons
1. Limited Geographical Spread: South India aur Punjab shaant rahe — revolt sirf North India mein zyada tha.
2. Limited Social Base: Educated middle class, rich merchants, aur zamindars ne saath nahi diya.
3. Lack of Central Leadership: Koi ek strong leader nahi tha — Bahadur Shah Zafar weak the, sirf symbolic figure the.
4. Poor Resources: British ke paas better guns, telegraph aur railways the → communication + logistics advantage.
South India + Punjab = shaant rahe No central leader British: telegraph + railways = advantage Middle class + zamindars = support nahi diya

Trap #1 — Mangal Pandey ne kisko maara?
Sahi naam: Lt. Baugh aur Sergeant-Major Hewson. PDF mein "Major Hudson" tha — yeh galat hai. Hewson sahi hai. Verified sources se confirm hai.
Trap #2 — Revolt kab shuru hua?
Mangal Pandey incident: 29 March 1857 (Barrackpore).
Asli / official revolt start: 10 May 1857 (Meerut).
Delhi rebels pahunche: 11 May 1857. Teeno dates alag hain.
Trap #3 — Real command Delhi mein kaun tha?
Bahadur Shah Zafar sirf symbolic leader tha. Real military command = General Bakht Khan. Dono alag hain.
Trap #4 — Historians ke views confuse mat karo
Savarkar = "War of Independence" (pehle)
R.C. Majumdar = "Neither first, nor national, nor war of independence"
Disraeli = "National Revolt" (British MP tha!)
T.R. Holmes = "Civilisation vs Barbarism"
Trap #5 — Jhansi waali quote
Hugh Rose ne Rani Laxmibai ke baare mein kaha: "The only man among rebels." Yeh Hugh Rose ka quote hai — British commander ka. Exam mein exactly yahi poochha jaata hai.
Trap #6 — Kunwar Singh ki umar
Kunwar Singh (Arrah/Jagdishpur) — Bihar ka hero — ~80 saal ke the jab revolt mein ladi. Oldest rebel leader. Yeh fact bahut poochha jaata hai.
Trap #7 — Revolt kab khatam hua?
Revolt start: 10 May 1857 (Meerut). Revolt khatam: 20 June 1858 (Gwalior). Government of India Act: 1858. Lord Canning = Governor-General during revolt + 1st Viceroy after.
Trap #8 — Doctrine of Lapse states
Exam mein examples poochhe jaate hain: Satara (1848), Sambalpur (1849), Jhansi (1853). Jhansi sabse famous hai — Rani Laxmibai ka connection isliye tha.
