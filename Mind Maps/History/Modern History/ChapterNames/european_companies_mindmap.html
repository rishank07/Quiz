📝 Exam Note

Swedish ka BPSC/BSSC mein directly poochha jaana unlikely hai — lekin agar "which European company last came" ya comparison question aaye toh yaad rakho: Swedish 1731 sabse last mein aaya.

⚠ Trap #1 — Establishment vs Factory in India
Dutch VOC (1602) English EIC (1600) ke baad bani, lekin India mein Dutch ki factory (1605) English ki factory (1613) se pehle lagi! Yahi most common confusion hai.
⚠ Trap #2 — Portuguese = State, Company Nahi
Portuguese ek State power tha, koi trading company nahi. Baaki sab (EIC, VOC, French, Danish) formal companies the. Portuguese ka "est." = 1498 arrival, koi company formation date nahi.
⚠ Trap #3 — Houtman India Nahi Gaya
Dutch traveler Houtman (1596) ne Indonesia (Sumatra) visit kiya tha, India nahi. Bahut students yahan galat karte hain.
⚠ Trap #4 — 1st Factory in India Kaun Si?
1st European factory = Portuguese, Cochin, 1503.
1st English temporary factory = Masulipatnam 1611.
1st English permanent factory = Surat 1613 (Thomas Aldworth).
⚠ Trap #5 — Vasco da Gama Ki Teen Trips
1st visit: 1498 — Calicut (Zamorin se mila).
2nd visit: 1502 — re-arrival.
3rd visit: 1524 — Cochin mein mar gaya.
⚠ Trap #6 — Carnatic Wars Ki Count
French vs British = Teen (3) Carnatic Wars, 1746–1763. French teeno mein haare. Count galat mat karna.
⚠ Trap #7 — Danish Ka Exit
Danish battle mein nahi haara — factories British ko bech di 1845 mein. Dutch haare battle mein (Bedara 1759), Danish ne peacefully becha. Dono alag hain!
