Cripps Mission — March 1942
📅
Announced: Early March 1942. Sent by: PM Winston Churchill. Head: Stafford Cripps. Arrived: 22 March 1942.
🤝
Negotiators: Congress — Jawaharlal Nehru + Maulana Azad. ML — Jinnah.
📋
Offer: After war Dominion Status + Constituent Assembly.
⚠️
Sabse Bada Trap — Opt-out Clause: "Agar koi state India ke saath nahi rehna chahti, toh alag ho sakti hai" → India ke tukde karne ki permission.
❌
Result: Congress + ML dono ne reject kiya. Gandhi ji ne kaha — "Post-dated cheque on a failing bank."

📋

❌

Quit India Movement — 1942
"Do or Die" — Karo ya Maro
Build-up & Launch
📅
Wardha Resolution: 14 July 1942 — Congress ne "Quit India Resolution" pass karne ka decide kiya.
📍
Launch: 8 August 1942 — Gowalia Tank Maidan (aaj = August Kranti Maidan), Mumbai.
⚡
Mass Phase: 9 August — sab bade neta arrested → Operation Zero Hour.
📢
Slogan: Gandhi ji — "Do or Die" (Karo ya Maro).
Government Action
🏛️
Gandhi ji ki Jail: Agha Khan Palace, Pune (Yerwada nahi!).
👩
Aruna Asaf Ali ne Gowalia Tank mein Tiranga fehraya jab saare mard neta andar the.
📻
Congress Radio (14 Aug 1942): Usha Mehta ne Bombay mein chhup kar radio station chalaya.
🏃
JP Narayan: Hazaribagh Central Jail se Diwali ki raat diwar faand kar bhage → jungle mein Azad Dasta banaya.
❌
Muslim League — Neutral rahi (support nahi kiya). Hindu Mahasabha — support nahi kiya.
Parallel Governments (Samanantar Sarkarein)
Place Leader Key Fact

Mountbatten Plan (3 June Plan) — 1947
📅

📋

📅

📋
Provisions: 2 Dominions (India + Pakistan). Governor-General har dominion ke liye. British Crown ki authority Princely States par khatam → freedom to join India/Pakistan.

⚠️ Trap: Liaqat Ali Khan = Finance
Liaqat Ali Khan (ML) ko Finance portfolio mila — ek Muslim League member ko itna powerful ministry milna unusual tha aur exams mein aksar poochha jaata hai.
⚠️ Trap #1 — Gandhi ki jail = Agha Khan Palace (Yerwada nahi)
Quit India Movement (1942) mein Gandhi ji ko Agha Khan Palace, Pune mein rakha gaya. Yerwada Jail = 1930 CDM mein tha. Dono alag hain!
⚠️ Trap #2 — Individual Satyagraha sequence
1st = Vinoba Bhave, 2nd = Jawaharlal Nehru, 3rd = Brahma Dutt. State exams mein 3rd kaun tha poochha jaata hai — Brahma Dutt.
⚠️ Trap #3 — INA Founder vs Supreme Commander
Founder: Captain Mohan Singh (1942). Supreme Commander: Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose (July 1943 se). Dono alag hain!
⚠️ Trap #4 — Pakistan naam vs idea
Idea (1930): Muhammad Iqbal — Allahabad Session. Naam "Pakistan" (1933): Choudhary Rahmat Ali. Official demand (1940): Jinnah — Lahore Session. Teeno alag log hain!
⚠️ Trap #5 — Liaqat Ali Khan = Finance (ML ka member)
Interim Govt mein Finance portfolio = Liaqat Ali Khan — jo Muslim League ka member tha. Congress ka member nahi.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Gowalia Tank = August Kranti Maidan
Quit India Movement launch jagah = Gowalia Tank Maidan, Mumbai = aaj August Kranti Maidan kehte hain. Dono same jagah ke alag naam hain.
⚠️ Trap #7 — Cabinet Mission members: "LCA"
Cabinet Mission = Pethick Lawrence + Cripps + A.V. Alexander. Cripps dono missions mein — 1942 Cripps Mission aur 1946 Cabinet Mission mein tha. Confuse mat karo.
⚠️ Trap #8 — Independence Act dates
Bill introduced: 4 July 1947. Passed (Royal Assent): 18 July 1947. Independence: 15 August 1947. Teeno dates alag hain.
⚠️ Trap #9 — Red Fort Trial main lawyer
Red Fort Trial (1945) main lawyer = Bhulabhai Desai. Nehru bhi tha, lekin Bhulabhai Desai ne lead kiya. Teeno accused: Dhillon (Sikh) + Prem Sehgal (Hindu) + Shahnawaz (Muslim).
⚠️ Trap #10 — Satara = sabse lambi parallel govt
Parallel Govts: Balia = sabse pehli. Satara = sabse lambi (1946 tak). Tamluk = Vidyut Vahini + cyclone help. Teeno alag facts.
