⚠️ Trap #1 — Khudiram Bose ka target vs actual victim
Target tha Kingsford (judge) — lekin bomb galti se Kennedy ki gaadi par gira aur Kingsford bach gaya. Kennedy ki gaadi alag thi. Yeh common confusion hai.
⚠️ Trap #2 — Tilak ki League = EXCEPT Bombay City
Tilak ki Home Rule League ka area = Karnataka + CP + Berar + Maharashtra — lekin Bombay City EXCEPT. Bombay City = Annie Besant ka area tha.
⚠️ Trap #3 — Ghadar Party: Founder vs President
Founder: Lala Hardayal. President: Sohan Singh Bhakna. Dono alag hain — mix mat karna.
⚠️ Trap #4 — Mitra Mela vs Abhinav Bharat
Mitra Mela (1899) → 1904 mein rename hokar Abhinav Bharat ban gaya. Inspired by Mazzini ka "Young Italy." Founder: V.D. Savarkar.
⚠️ Trap #5 — Partition cancel kab hua?
Bengal Partition 1905 mein laagu hua. 1911 mein Delhi Durbar mein cancel hua (Lord Hardinge II ke time). Curzon ne partition kiya, Hardinge ne cancel.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Capital shift announcement vs actual shift
Capital shift announce hua 1911 (Delhi Durbar). Official Delhi capital ban gaya 1912 mein.
⚠️ Trap #7 — Prafulla Chaki vs Khudiram Bose ka fate
Prafulla Chaki = pakde jaane ke darr se suicide kiya (khud ko goli mari).
Khudiram Bose = arrest aur 11 August 1908 ko phaansi. Youngest revolutionary hanged (~18 years).
⚠️ Trap #8 — Anushilan Samiti founder vs Dhaka branch
Anushilan Samiti 1902 = Pramathanath Mitra (P. Mitra) Calcutta mein.
Dhaka Anushilan Samiti 1905 = Pulin Das (P. Mitra se inspired) Dhaka mein. Dono alag hain!
⚠️ Trap #9 — First Tiranga unfurled
First Indian Tricolour (Tiranga) = Madam Bhikaji Cama ne Stuttgart, Germany mein 1907 mein unfurled kiya — International Socialist Congress mein. India mein nahi!
⚠️ Trap #10 — Aurobindo Ghosh ka ashram kahan hai?
Sri Aurobindo Ashram = Puducherry (Pondicherry), established 1926. Newspaper: Karmayogin (1909). Alipore case ke baad politics chhodkar gaye.
