⚠️ Trap #1 — Plassey vs Buxar commander
Plassey (1757): Robert Clive ka tha. Buxar (1764): Hector Munro ka tha. Dono alag hain — Robert Clive ko Buxar mein mat dena!
⚠️ Trap #2 — Black Hole numbers
Exam mein Holwell's version use hota hai: 146 imprisoned, 23 survived. Modern historians ka estimate alag hai (~64 imprisoned, ~43 died) — but BPSC/BSSC exam context mein Holwell's figures accept karo.
⚠️ Trap #3 — Mir Jafar 2 baar Nawab bana
Mir Jafar do baar Bengal ka Nawab tha: 1757–1760 aur phir 1763–1765. Beech mein Mir Qasim tha (1760–1763). Sequence yaad rakho.
⚠️ Trap #4 — Carnatic Wars ki treaties
1st → Aix-la-Chapelle 1748 (status quo)
2nd → Pondicherry 1755 (Dupleix recall)
3rd → Paris 1763 (French power khatam)
⚠️ Trap #5 — Bombay kaisi mili?
Bombay conquest mein nahi, dowry mein mila — 1661 mein Charles II ki shaadi se. Portuguese Princess Catherine of Braganza ne dowry mein di.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Anglo-Mysore Wars ki GGs
1st: No GG mentioned
2nd: Warren Hastings — Mangalore 1784
3rd: Cornwallis — Seringapatam 1792
4th: Wellesley — Tipu killed 1799
⚠️ Trap #7 — Wandiwash (1760) commanders
British: Eyre Coote (won). French: Count de Lally (haara). Yeh 3rd Carnatic War mein tha — exact names poochhe jaate hain.
⚠️ Trap #8 — Anglo-Afghan 1st War result
1st Afghan War (1839–1842) British ke liye disastrous tha — Kabul se retreat karna pada. Sindh 1843 mein annex hua (alag event, same time period).
