Swaraj Party — Formation & Context
Background — Gaya Congress 1922
👤
President: Chittaranjan Das (C.R. Das).
📋
Proposal: C.R. Das + Motilal Nehru — "Congress Legislative Councils mein ghuse aur andar se virodh kare."
❌
Result: Congress ne proposal reject kar diya → C.R. Das ne alag party bana li.
Swaraj Party Details
📅
Founded: January 1923. Place: Prayagraj (Allahabad).
📛
Earlier Name: Congress-Khilafat Swaraj Party.
👑
President: C.R. Das. Secretary: Motilal Nehru.
🎯
Objective: Councils mein entry lekar Colonial Governance ko obstruct karna, budget debates ka use karna, Responsible Government ke liye pressure.
Political Dualism — 2 Factions
No-Changers
Ideology: Constructive work — schools, ashrams banana. Council entry ke khilaf.
Leaders: C. Rajagopalachari, Vallabhbhai Patel
Pro-Changers (Swarajists)
Ideology: Legislative Council mein ghusna aur andar se obstruct karna.
Leaders: C.R. Das, Motilal Nehru, Vitthalbhai Patel
⭐
Biggest Achievement (1925): Vitthalbhai Patel became President (Speaker) of Central Legislative Assembly — Swarajists ki sabse badi jeet.
Swaraj Party Jan 1923 = Prayagraj C.R. Das = President | Motilal Nehru = Secretary Vitthalbhai Patel 1925 = Speaker CLA No-Changers = Rajagopalachari + Vallabhbhai

HRA → HSRA — Revolutionary Organizations
HRA — Hindustan Republican Association
Founded: 1924, Kanpur
Main Leader: Sachindranath Sanyal
Objective: Armed Revolution. Federal Republic of India (United States of India).
Inspired by: Irish + Russian revolutionary traditions
Publications: "The Revolutionary" (manifesto), "Philosophy of the Bomb" (Bhagwati Charan Vohra)

HSRA — Hindustan Socialist Republican Association
Founded: 10 December 1928
Place: Feroz Shah Kotla Ground, Delhi
Key Leadership: Chandrashekhar Azad + Bhagat Singh
Objective: India mein Socialist Republican State banana
Note: HRA ka naam badal kar HSRA kiya — "Socialist" add hua
Other Members: Bhagwati Charan Vohra, Sukhdev, Vijay Kumar Sinha, Shiv Kumar, Jaydev Kapoor

⚠️ Trap #1 — CPI ki 2 formation dates
CPI abroad = 17 Oct 1920, Tashkent (M.N. Roy). CPI India mein = 26 Dec 1925, Kanpur (Satyabhakta). Dono dates alag hain — question mein "where" dekho.
⚠️ Trap #2 — Saunders ka case: Target vs Actual victim
Asal target tha James Scott (lathi charge order dene wala). Lekin galti se John Saunders (Assistant SP) maara gaya. Bhagat Singh, Azad, Rajguru involve the.
⚠️ Trap #3 — Shaheed Diwas = 23 March 1931
Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev, Rajguru ko phaansi = 23 March 1931. Chandrashekhar Azad = 27 February 1931. Dono alag dates hain.
⚠️ Trap #4 — Vitthalbhai vs Vallabhbhai Patel
Vitthalbhai Patel (bade bhai) = 1925 mein Speaker of Central Legislative Assembly. Vallabhbhai Patel (chote bhai) = "Sardar" — Bardoli Satyagraha wale. Dono bhai hain, roles alag!
⚠️ Trap #5 — Assembly Bomb mein 2 bills yaad rakho
Assembly Bomb (8 Apr 1929) ka protest tha: (1) Public Safety Bill + (2) Trade Disputes Bill. Sirf ek mat bolna — dono yaad rakho.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Bande Mataram = Bipin Pal, New India = Annie Besant
Bande Mataram journal = Bipin Chandra Pal. New India = Annie Besant. Dono confuse karte hain exam mein.
⚠️ Trap #7 — Jatin Das ki hunger strike = 64 days, 13 September
Jatin Das ne 64-day hunger strike ki — death 13 September 1929. December nahi, September. Jail mein political prisoner status ke liye.
⚠️ Trap #8 — HRA vs HSRA alag hain
HRA (1924) = Sachindranath Sanyal, Kanpur. HSRA (10 Dec 1928) = Bhagat Singh + Azad, Feroz Shah Kotla Delhi. HRA rename hokar HSRA bana — "Socialist" add hua.
⚠️ Trap #9 — AITUC ki 1st presidency
AITUC 1920 First President = Lala Lajpat Rai. INTUC 1947 First President = Vallabhbhai Patel. Dono alag unions hain.
⚠️ Trap #10 — Bandi Jivan = "Bible of Revolutionaries"
Bandi Jivan = Sachindranath Sanyal ki book — "Bible of Revolutionaries" kehte hain. Gita Rahasya = Tilak, Mandalay Jail mein likhi. Dono important books — alag hain.
