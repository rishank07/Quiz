⚠️ Trap #1 — Champaran = 1st Gandhian Satyagraha
Champaran (1917) Gandhi ka pehla satyagraha tha — Bihar mein. "Tinkathia System" ke against tha (indigo farmers). Kheda (1918) Gujarat mein tha — dono alag hain.
⚠️ Trap #2 — Bardoli = Sardar ka title
Bardoli Satyagraha (1928) mein Vallabhbhai Patel ki jeet ke baad unhein "Sardar" ka title mila — Gujarat ki mahilaon ne diya. Leader = Patel, not Gandhi.
⚠️ Trap #3 — Santhal vs Munda dono alag hain
Santhal Rebellion (Hul) = 1855–56, leaders: Sidhu & Kanhu, region: Bengal/Jharkhand.
Munda Rebellion (Ulgulan) = 1899–1900, leader: Birsa Munda, region: Jharkhand.
"Hul" = revolt (Santali). "Ulgulan" = great tumult (Mundari). Dono different hain!
⚠️ Trap #4 — Tebhaga ka demand
Tebhaga = sharecroppers ko 2/3rd share milna chahiye — 1/3rd landlord ko. Pehle half-half tha. "Tebhaga" = three shares (Bengali). Led by BPKS / All India Kisan Sabha (CPI).
⚠️ Trap #5 — Paika Rebellion ka region
Paika Rebellion (1817) = Odisha mein, leader = Bakshi Jagabandhu. Issue = land revenue + salt monopoly. Sambalpur Rebellion se alag hai — wo Surendra Sai (1827–62) ka tha.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Indigo Movement ka sahi naam
Indigo Movement (1859–60) Bengal mein tha — Neel Vidroh bhi kehte hain. Leader: Digambar Biswas. Champaran (1917) se alag — wo Gandhi ka tha. Dono indigo se related par alag hain!
⚠️ Trap #7 — Moplah Rebellion ke 3 causes
Moplah Rebellion (1921–22, Kerala) ke teen causes the: Khilafat movement + anti-landlord + anti-British. Sirf ek mat batana.
⚠️ Trap #8 — Rani Gaidinliu + Rani Chennamma alag hain
Rani Chennamma = Kittur Rebellion 1824–29, Karnataka.
Rani Gaidinliu = Naga Rebellion 1931, Nagaland. "Rani" dono ke naam mein hai — confuse mat karna!
⚠️ Trap #9 — Kuka vs Faraizi Movement
Kuka Movement (1860–70) = Punjab, leader: Bhagat Jawahar Mal, Namdhari Sikh movement.
Faraizi Movement (1818–60) = Bengal, leader: Haji Shariatullah, Muslim reform movement.
