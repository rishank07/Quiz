Bengal Region — 6 Major Organizations
01
Bang Bhasha Prakashak Sabha
1836
Founders: Raja Ram Mohan Roy ke followers
Promote Bengali language + literature. Review government administration.
02
Landholders' Society
1838, Calcutta
Founder: Dwarkanath Tagore
⭐ India ka Pehla Organized Political Association. Protect zamindars' interests. Used constitutional methods.
03
Bengal British India Society
1843
Founders: George Thompson & Peary Chand Mitra
Indians + non-official British. "Constitutional Agitation" — badhana Indians ki participation in British administration.
04
British Indian Association
28 Oct 1851
Founders: Radhakanta Dev (President) + Debendranath Tagore (Secretary)
Merger of: Landholders' Society + Bengal British India Society. Petitions & memoranda to Parliament.
05
Indian League
Sep 1875
Founder: Shishir Kumar Ghosh (Editor, Amrit Bazar Patrika)
Temp President: Shambhu Chandra Mukherjee
Instill nationalism. Political education encourage karna.
06
Indian National Association
26 Jul 1876
Place: Albert Hall, Calcutta
Founders: Surendranath Banerjee + Anand Mohan Bose
⭐ Most Important Pre-Congress Org. First All-India political platform. 1886 mein INC mein merge hua. Surendranath = "Rashtra Guru" + "King without Crown."

Indian National Congress — Establishment
28 December 1885 | Bombay
📅
Date: 28th December 1885.
📍
Place: Gokuldas Tejpal Sanskrit College, Bombay (near Gowalia Tank).
👤
Founder: Allan Octavian Hume (A.O. Hume) — British Civil Servant.
👑
First President: Womesh Chandra Bonnerjee (W.C. Bonnerjee).
🏛️
Viceroy at that time: Lord Dufferin.
First Session Stats
👥
Total Delegates: 72. Secretary: A.O. Hume (Organising Secretary).
🔢
Breakdown: Hindus (majority — 54), Muslims: 2, Others: Parsis, Jains, Christians.
📊
Region-wise: Bombay: 38 (max) → Madras: 21 → UP/Awadh: 7 → Bengal: 3 → Punjab: 3.
3 Theories of INC Formation
🛡️
Safety Valve Theory — A.O. Hume: Ek platform jo Indians ka gussa release kare taaki 1857 jaisa revolt na ho.
⚡
Lightning Conductor Theory — Gopal Krishna Gokhale: Indians ne Hume ko use kiya taaki British Congress ko shuru mein crush na kare.
🕵️
Conspiracy Theory — Rajani Palme Dutt (R.P. Dutt): Yeh British ki ek saazish thi.
28 Dec 1885 = INC founded A.O. Hume = Founder (British) W.C. Bonnerjee = 1st President Lord Dufferin = Viceroy
1st Session 1885 Bombay
President: Womesh Chandra Bonnerjee | Secretary: A.O. Hume
72 delegates. Coordination banana + National Unity. Bombay se max 38 delegates.
2nd Session 1886 Calcutta
President: Dadabhai Naoroji
Key Fact: First Parsi President. Members: 434 (grew from 72).
3rd Session 1887 Madras
President: Syed Badruddin Tyabji
Key Fact: First Muslim President of Congress. "Subject Determination Committee" banayi gayi.
4th Session 1888 Prayagraj (Allahabad)
President: George Yule
Key Fact: First English/British President. Lala Lajpat Rai ne pehli baar isi session mein participate kiya.
12th Session 1896 Calcutta
President: Rahimtulla Sayani
🎵 Pehli baar "Vande Mataram" gaya gaya!
Composer: Bankim Chandra Chatterjee (Anandamath, 1882). Singer: Rabindranath Tagore.
22nd Session 1906 Calcutta
President: Dadabhai Naoroji
Pehli baar "Swaraj" shabd use hua. 4 Resolutions passed:
1. Swaraj (Self-rule) as goal. 2. Boycott Movement. 3. Swadeshi. 4. National Education.
23rd Session 1907 Surat
President: Rash Bihari Ghosh
💥 FIRST SPLIT OF CONGRESS — Surat Split!
Moderates (Naram Dal) vs Extremists (Garam Dal). Reason: 1906 ke 4 resolutions — Extremists chahte the poore India mein failayein, Moderates sirf Bengal tak seemit rakhna chahte the.
26th Session 1911 Calcutta
President: Pandit Bishan Narayan Dhar
🎵 Pehli baar "Jana Gana Mana" (National Anthem) gaya gaya!
Composer: Rabindranath Tagore.
31st Session 1916 Lucknow
President: Ambika Charan Majumdar
1. Patch-up: Extremists + Moderates wapas mile.
2. Lucknow Pact: Congress + Muslim League agreement. (Luck khul gaya!)
32nd Session 1917 Calcutta
President: Annie Besant
⭐ First Female President of Congress (videshi / foreign). Note: First Indian Female = Sarojini Naidu (1925).
39th Session 1924 Belgaum (Karnataka)
President: Mahatma Gandhi
⭐ Gandhi ji ne sirf ek baar Congress ki adhyakshta ki — yahi session.
44th Session 1929 Lahore (Ravi river ke tat par)
President: Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru
⭐ "Purna Swaraj" resolution passed (Complete Independence). Decided: 26 January 1930 ko pehla Independence Day manaya jayega.
45th Session 1931 Karachi
President: Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
Resolution: "Fundamental Rights" + "National Economic Programme" passed.

Dadabhai Naoroji — Grand Old Man of India
Category Details
Birth / Place 4 September 1825, Navsari, Gujarat
Title "Grand Old Man of India"
INC Role Earliest economic critic of British rule. Laid ideological foundation of early nationalism.
Famous Theory "Drain of Wealth Theory" (Dhan Nishkasan ka siddhant)
Famous Book "Poverty and Un-British Rule in India"
Economy First Indian to calculate National Income
Magazine "Rast Goftar" (Gujarati — "Truth Teller")
Swaraj First user of word "Swaraj" — Calcutta Session, 1906 (as INC President)
Grand Old Man of India Drain of Wealth Theory 1st to calculate National Income 2nd INC President (1886) = 1st Parsi East India Association 1866 = Dadabhai

Memory Tricks
🔥 Surat Split 1907
"Surat mein Congress ki Surat bigad gayi." (Split ho gaya).
🍀 Lucknow Pact 1916
"Lucknow mein Luck khul gaya." (Sab mil gaye — Moderates + Extremists + Muslim League).
🌿 Gandhi 1924 Belgaum
Gandhi ji sirf ek baar — 1924 Belgaum mein. Yaad rakho: 1924 = Ek baar hi.
👩 Females — 2 hain, dono alag
1917: Annie Besant (Videshi — British). 1925: Sarojini Naidu (Desi — Indian). 8 saal ka gap.
⚠️ Trap #1 — 1st Female vs 1st Indian Female President
First Female President = Annie Besant (1917, British) — Videshi thi.
First Indian Female President = Sarojini Naidu (1925, Kanpur) — Desi. 8 saal ka gap.
⚠️ Trap #2 — Vande Mataram composer vs singer
Composer (Writer): Bankim Chandra Chatterjee (in novel Anandamath, 1882).
First Singer (1896 INC session): Rabindranath Tagore. Dono alag roles!
⚠️ Trap #3 — Jana Gana Mana vs Vande Mataram sessions
Vande Mataram = 12th Session, 1896 Calcutta (Sayani presided).
Jana Gana Mana = 26th Session, 1911 Calcutta (Bishan Narayan Dhar presided). Dono Calcutta mein! Par alag saal.
⚠️ Trap #4 — Surat Split (1907) ka president
Surat Split 1907 ka President = Rash Bihari Ghosh. Tilak ya Lal-Bal-Pal nahi — woh leaders the, president nahi. Sirf president yaad rakho.
⚠️ Trap #5 — Pehla Organized Political Association
India ka Pehla Organized Political Association = Landholders' Society (1838), founder: Dwarkanath Tagore. Indian National Association (1876) ko confuse mat karo.
⚠️ Trap #6 — Dadabhai Naoroji = "Swaraj" first use
Word "Swaraj" pehli baar Dadabhai Naoroji ne use kiya — 22nd Session (1906) Calcutta mein, jab woh President the. Gokhale ya Tilak ne nahi.
⚠️ Trap #7 — Lightning Conductor = Gokhale, Safety Valve = Hume
Safety Valve Theory = A.O. Hume ne diya.
Lightning Conductor Theory = Gopal Krishna Gokhale ne diya.
Conspiracy Theory = R.P. Dutt ne diya. Teeno alag hain!
⚠️ Trap #8 — Indian National Association merge hua INC mein
Indian National Association (1876, Surendranath Banerjee) — Most Important Pre-Congress org — 1886 mein INC mein merge ho gayi. Yeh pehli All-India political platform thi.
⚠️ Trap #9 — Muslim League founder vs 1st President
Muslim League (1906, Dhaka):
Initiative: Nawab Salimullah of Dhaka.
First President: Aga Khan.
HQ: Lucknow. Teeno alag hain — mix mat karna.
⚠️ Trap #10 — Lucknow Session 1916 mein 2 events
31st Session (1916, Lucknow) mein 2 events: (1) Moderates + Extremists patch-up. (2) Lucknow Pact — Congress + Muslim League agreement. President: Ambika Charan Majumdar (Tilak nahi).
