Bhakti Movement — Overview
  • Period: 7th – 17th Century
  • Monotheistic — worship of one God
  • Rejection of rituals and sacrifices
  • Opposition to caste system
  • Emphasis on Bhakti (devotion) towards God
Two Branches of Bhakti
🌸 Saguna (With Form)
  • Worships God with attributes / form
  • Shiva worshippers → Nayanar
  • Vishnu worshippers → Alvar
  • Only female Alvar: Andal
  • Saints: Ramananda, Surdas, Meerabai, Tulsidas, Chaitanya
✨ Nirguna (Formless)
  • Worships formless / attributeless God
  • Saints: Kabirdas, Guru Nanak, Dadu Dayal
  • Emphasis on inner devotion over rituals

⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — Saguna vs Nirguna

Kabirdas aur Guru Nanak = Nirguna (formless God). Ramananda, Meerabai, Surdas, Tulsidas = Saguna. Kabirdas ko Saguna mat kaho — yeh common mistake hai. Ramananda Saguna the lekin unke student Kabir Nirguna the.

Trap #2 — Meerabai's Guru

Meerabai ka Guru = Raidas (Ravidas) — jo Ramananda ka disciple tha. Ramananda directly Meerabai ka guru nahi tha.

Trap #3 — Only Female Alvar

Vishnu-worshipping Alvar saints mein ek hi mahila Alvar thi = Andal. Nayanar (Shiva) ke female saints alag hain.

Trap #4 — Bhavartha-Deepika vs Bhavarth Ramayana

Bhavartha-Deepika (Gita commentary on Marathi) = Gyaneshwar. Bhavarth Ramayana (Ramayana commentary) = Eknath. Dono alag works, alag authors.

Trap #5 — Chishti Order: Oldest, not Largest

Chishti Order = Oldest Sufi Order in India — largest nahi. Founder Muinuddin Chishti ka Khanqah Ajmer mein tha, Delhi mein nahi.

Trap #6 — Nizamuddin Auliya aur Amir Khusrau

Nizamuddin Auliya = Chishti order. Amir Khusrau unka disciple (Murid) tha — poet aur musician. Nizamuddin ko Mehboob-e-Ilahi kaha jata hai.

Trap #7 — Tansen's Guru

Tansen ka Guru = Muhammad Ghaus (Shattari Order) — Nizamuddin Auliya nahi. Muhammad Ghaus ko 'Qutb' bhi kaha jata tha.

Trap #8 — Sattriya Dance Founder

Sattriya dance form ka founder = Shankaradeva (Assam) — Bharatnatyam ya Kathak se mat confuse karo. Shankaradeva ne Bhakti movement bhi Assam mein start ki aur Borgeets tradition bhi.
