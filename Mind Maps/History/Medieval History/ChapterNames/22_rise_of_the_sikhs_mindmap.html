Guru 1–5 — Key Facts
1Guru Nanak Dev Ji
1469–1539 CE
  • Founder of Sikhism
  • Born: Talwandi (Nankana Sahib, Pakistan) | Death: Kartarpur
  • Father: Kalu | Mother: Tripta Devi | Wife: Sulakhni Devi
  • Sons: Shri Chand, Lakshmi Chand
  • Knowledge on banks of: Kali Bein River
  • Founded: Sangat (congregation) + Pangat/Langar (community kitchen)
  • Philosophy: Monotheism
  • Teachings in: Guru Granth Sahib (Nanakavani)
  • Famous hymns: Japji, Sohila, Rehras
2Guru Angad Dev Ji (Lehna)
1539–1552 CE
  • Initiated the Gurmukhi script
  • Regularised the Langar system
3Guru Amar Das Ji
1552–1574 CE
  • Preached on family life
  • Contemporary of Akbar
  • Received 500 bigha land from Akbar
4Guru Ram Das Ji
1574–1581 CE
  • Founded Amritsar city
  • Made Guru position hereditary
5Guru Arjan Dev Ji
1581–1606 CE
  • Founded Tarn Taran town
  • Compiled Adi Granth (Guru Granth Sahib)
  • Constructed Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) in Amritsar sarovar
  • Encouraged 1/10th earnings contribution (Dasvandh)
  • Executed by Jahangir in 1606 CE (for supporting Khusrau's revolt)

⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — Guru Arjan Dev — Executed by Whom?

Guru Arjan Dev (5th Guru) ko Jahangir ne execute kiya (1606 CE) — Aurangzeb ne nahi. Guru Tegh Bahadur (9th) ko Aurangzeb ne execute kiya. Dono alag hain.

Trap #2 — Gurmukhi Script — Kaun?

Gurmukhi script = Guru Angad Dev Ji (2nd Guru) ne initiate kiya — Guru Nanak ne nahi. Guru Nanak ne Sikhism found kiya, script baad mein bani.

Trap #3 — Amritsar City Founded by?

Amritsar city = Guru Ram Das Ji (4th Guru) ne found ki. Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) = Guru Arjan Dev Ji (5th Guru) ne banaya. City aur Temple ke founders alag hain.

Trap #4 — Only Elected Guru

Only elected Sikh Guru = Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji (9th) — baki sab hereditary/appointed the. Guru Ram Das ne Guru position hereditary banaya (4th Guru se).

Trap #5 — Khalsa Panth Year

Khalsa Panth founded = 1699 CE by Guru Gobind Singh (10th Guru). Dal Khalsa = 1748 CE by Kapoor Singh (alag cheez). Dono ko mix mat karo.

Trap #6 — 2 Treaties Named "Treaty of Amritsar"

Treaty of Amritsar (1809) = Ranjit Singh + Charles Metcalfe (Sutlej boundary). Treaty of Amritsar = 2nd Anglo-Sikh War (1848–49) — Kohinoor lia. Dono alag contexts mein — year se identify karo.
