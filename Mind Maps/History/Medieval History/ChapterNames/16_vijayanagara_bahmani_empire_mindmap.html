⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — Vijayanagara Founders

Founders = Harihara I aur Bukka — dono founders hain, sirf ek nahi. Yeh Kampili kingdom ke ministers the pehle. 1336 CE mein empire banaya.

Trap #2 — Religion: Shaivism vs Vaishnavism

Vijayanagara ka state religion = Shaivism. Lekin Krishna Deva Raya personally Vaishnavism follow karte the. Dono alag hain — confuse mat karo.

Trap #3 — Battle of Talikota — Who Fought?

Vijayanagara side se ruler = Sadasiva Raya lekin actual control Rama Raya (minister) ke haath mein tha. Confederation = 4 sultanates (Ahmadnagar, Bijapur, Golkonda, Bidar) — Bahmani ek nahi tha.

Trap #4 — Allasani Peddana's Title

Allasani Peddana = "Grandfather of Telugu poetry" (Andhra Kavita Pitamaha). Krishna Deva Raya ko "Andhra Pitamaha" kaha jata hai — dono alag hain.

Trap #5 — Bahmani Founder's Names

Bahmani founder ke 3 names: Original = Zafar Khan, Nickname = Hasan Gangu, Ruler name = Alauddin Hasan Bahman Shah. Teen names, ek hi aadmi.

Trap #6 — Capital Shift (Bahmani)

Bahmani capital pehle Gulbarga (Ahsanabad) tha — founder Alauddin Hasan ne. Phir Shihabuddin Ahmad Shah I ne Bidar shift kiya. Aksar exam mein galat ruler se link karte hain.

Trap #7 — Hyderabad & Charminar

Hyderabad city ka founder = Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah — ne Charminar bhi banaya. Bahmani dynasty se alag — yeh Qutb Shahi dynasty (Golkonda) tha.

Trap #8 — Gol Gumbaz Builder

Gol Gumbaz = Muhammad Adil Shah (Bijapur) ne banaya — Ibrahim Adil Shah ne nahi. Ibrahim ne Kitab-e-Nauras likhi aur Dakhani language promote ki.
