⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — Shivaji ki 2 Coronations

Shivaji ki do coronations hui: 6 June 1674 aur 24 September 1674 — ek nahi. Dono same year mein. Scholar: Gaga Bhatta (Kashi).

Trap #2 — Treaty of Purandar — Do Alag Treaties

Treaty of Purandar = 2 alag treaties: (1) 1665 CE — Shivaji + Jai Singh. 23/35 forts to Mughals. (2) 1776 CE — Madhavrao Narayan + British. Dono alag hain.

Trap #3 — Chauth vs Sardeshmukhi

Chauth = 1/4 (25%) share — from neighbouring states for non-aggression. Sardeshmukhi = 1/10 (10%) tribute. Dono alag taxes — confuse mat karo.

Trap #4 — Magna Carta of Marathas

Magna Carta of Marathas = Delhi treaty between Balaji Vishwanath and Sayyid Brothers — NOT Treaty of Purandar. Aksar log confuse karte hain.

Trap #5 — 3rd Battle of Panipat — Date

3rd Battle of Panipat = 14 January 1761. Marathas defeated by Ahmad Shah Abdali (Afghanistan) — not Mughals. Peshwa at time = Balaji Baji Rao.

Trap #6 — Peshwa vs Ashtapradhan

Peshwa was 1 minister in the Ashtapradhan (Prime Minister role). Later, Peshwa became hereditary during Balaji Baji Rao's time and got supreme power — Chhatrapati puppet bane.

Trap #7 — Anglo-Maratha Wars GG

1st = Warren Hastings. 2nd = Lord Wellesley. 3rd = Lord Hastings. Warren Hastings ≠ Lord Hastings — dono alag hain (different people, different wars).
