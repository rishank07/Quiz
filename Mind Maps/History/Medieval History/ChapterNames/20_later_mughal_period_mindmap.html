Bahadur Shah I1707–1712 CE
  • Became emperor at age 65
  • Followed conciliatory policy with Marathas and Rajputs
  • Dutch representative: Joshua Ketelaar arrived in Mughal court
  • Title: Shah-e-Bekhbar (The Unaware King)
  • Won Battle of Jajau (1707) to ascend throne
Jahandar Shah1712–1713 CE
  • Came to power with help of Zulfiqar Khan
  • Gave title Mirza to Raja Jai Singh I (Amer)
  • Gave title Maharaja to Raja Ajit Singh (Marwar)
  • Abolished Jizya tax on Hindus
  • Nicknames: Lampat Moorkh, Laparwah
Farrukhsiyar1713–1719 CE
  • Came to power with help of Sayyid Brothers
  • Issued royal Farman in 1717 CE in favour of East India Company
  • Restored Jizya tax
  • Nickname: Ghranit Kayar (Notorious Coward)
Sayyid Brothers
  • Controlled administration: 1713–1720 CE
  • Known as Kingmakers (Khargonebar)
  • Followed tolerance and reconciliation with Rajputs, Marathas, Jats

⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — Jizya: On-Off-On-Off

Jahandar Shah ne abolish kiya → Farrukhsiyar ne restore kiya → Muhammad Shah (1720) ne finally abolish kiya. "Finally abolished" = Muhammad Shah — koi aur nahi.

Trap #2 — Kingmakers = Sayyid Brothers

Sayyid Brothers = Kingmakers (Khargonebar) — yeh Farrukhsiyar ke time pe power mein aaye (1713–1720 CE). Tolerance policy follow ki. Farrukhsiyar ko bhi inhone power diya aur baad mein hataya.

Trap #3 — Nadir Shah ne kya liya?

Nadir Shah ne Peacock Throne (Takht-e-Taus) AND Kohinoor diamond — dono liye. Sirf ek nahi, dono. Battle = Karnal. Year = 1739 CE.

Trap #4 — Battle of Buxar vs Plassey

Battle of Buxar (1764) = Shah Alam II + Shuja-ud-Daula + Mir Qasim vs British (Hector Munro). Battle of Plassey (1757) = Siraj-ud-Daula vs Clive. Dono alag battles, alag commanders.

Trap #5 — Treaty of Allahabad — Diwani Rights

Treaty of Allahabad (1765 CE) mein EIC ko Bengal, Bihar aur Orissa ke Diwani rights mile. Robert Clive ne represent kiya. Shah Alam II ko pension mili. Yeh revenue collection ka right tha, not political control directly.

Trap #6 — Bahadur Shah II — Died Where?

Bahadur Shah II (Zafar) ko Revolt of 1857 ke baad arrest kiya gaya aur Rangoon (Myanmar) bheja gaya. Wahan 1862 CE mein mara. Delhi ya Agra mein nahi.
