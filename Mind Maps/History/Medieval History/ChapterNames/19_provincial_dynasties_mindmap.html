Why Provincial Dynasties Arose
  • After Tughlaq dynasty's decline — decentralization increased
  • First province to break free from Delhi Sultanate: Bengal (1338 CE)
  • Each province developed its own rulers, culture, architecture

⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — First Province to be Independent

Bengal (1338 CE) = first province to become independent from Delhi Sultanate. Jaunpur ya Malwa nahi — Bengal sabse pehle tha.

Trap #2 — Jaunpur City — Founded by vs Named after

Jaunpur city founded by Firoz Shah Tughlaq. But city named in memory of Muhammad bin Tughlaq (Jauna Khan). Dono alag hain. Jaunpur = Jauna + pur.

Trap #3 — Zain-ul-Abidin — Sati ban

Sikandar Shah (Butshikan) ne Sati ban kiya. Zain-ul-Abidin ne Sati ka ban revoke (hataya). Opposite actions — confuse mat karo. Zain = liberal, Sikandar = orthodox.

Trap #4 — "Akbar of Kashmir"

Zain-ul-Abidin = "Akbar of Kashmir" (liberal ruler). Title Budshah = The Great King. Real name/title dono yaad rakho.

Trap #5 — Gujarat Founder vs Real Founder

Zafar Khan (Muzzafar Shah I) = Founder (1407 CE). Ahmed Shah = Real Founder (1411 CE). Ahmed Shah ne Ahmedabad banaya — Zafar Khan ne nahi. Same Founder/Real Founder trap.

Trap #6 — Mahmud Begada — Title ka reason

Begada title mila kyunki usne do (2) forts jite: Girnar Durg (1466) + Champaner Durg (1486). Ek fort nahi — do forts se Begada bana (Be = 2, dono ka combo).

Trap #7 — Adina Mosque vs Atala Mosque

Adina Mosque (Bengal) = built by Sikandar Shah (Bengal). Atala Mosque (Jaunpur, 1408 CE) = built by Ibrahim Shah Sharqi. Dono alag provinces, alag builders.
