⚠️ Exam Traps
Trap #1 — First Muslim vs First Arab Invader

First Muslim invaders = Arabs (as a group). First individual Arab/Muslim invader = Muhammad bin Qasim (712 CE). "First Muslim" ka seedha jawab = Arabs ya Muhammad bin Qasim — context dekhna zaroori hai.

Trap #2 — Mahmud Ghazni ka objective

Mahmud Ghazni ka India invasion ka maqsad sirf wealth looting tha — permanent conquest nahi. Ghori ka maqsad permanent rule tha. Dono ka intention ALAG tha.

Trap #3 — 17 Invasions vs 16th Invasion

Mahmud Ghazni ne 17 invasions kiye total. Somnath Temple attack uski 16th invasion (1025–26 CE) thi — 17th nahi. Bahut aksar exam mein 17th likha hota hai — yeh galat hai.

Trap #4 — Bhim I vs Bhim II

Somnath Temple (1025–26 CE) ke time ruler = Bhim I (Gujarat) — who was defeated. Gujarat (1178 CE, Patan) mein Ghori ke against = Bhim II victorious. Dono alag hain — I aur II confuse mat karo.

Trap #5 — 1st vs 2nd Battle of Tarain

1191 CE (1st battle) = Prithviraj Chauhan jeeta. 1192 CE (2nd battle) = Ghori jeeta, Prithviraj haara. Reverse mat karo — yeh most common mistake hai.

Trap #6 — Nalanda Destroyer

Nalanda University ko destroy kiya Bakhtiyar Khilji ne — Muhammad Ghori ne ya Mahmud Ghazni ne nahi. Bakhtiyar = Bengal wala slave of Ghori.

Trap #7 — Al-Biruni ki Book

Al-Biruni ki book = Tahqiq-i-Hind (Kitab-ul-Hind bhi kahte hain). Utbi ki book = Kitab-ul-Yamini. Dono Mahmud Ghazni ke court mein the. Firdausi ka Shahnama bhi Mahmud ke court mein — lekin yeh Persian epic poetry hai, India ka history nahi.
