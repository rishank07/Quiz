🔀 The Great Split — Digambara vs Shwetambara ▼
⬜ Shwetambara (White-Clad)
Leader: Sthulabhadra
Region: Stayed in North India (Magadha)
Practice: Wore White Clothes
Belief: Women CAN attain Moksha
🌌 Digambara (Sky-Clad)
Leader: Bhadrabahu
Region: Migrated to South India (Karnataka)
Practice: Did NOT wear clothes (Naked)
Key Event: Chandragupta Maurya accompanied Bhadrabahu to Shravanabelagola → performed Sallekhana (death by fasting)

⚠ TRAP 01 — Founder vs Propagator
Mahavira = Actual Founder / Propagator of Jainism (24th Tirthankara)
Parshvanath = 23rd Tirthankara — gave 4 vows but did NOT found Jainism
"Jainism ke Sansthapak" puchha jaaye → Mahavira
⚠ TRAP 02 — 4 Vows vs 5 Vows
Parshvanath gave 4 vows: Ahimsa, Satya, Asteya, Aparigraha
Mahavira ADDED the 5th: Brahmacharya (Celibacy)
Exam mein "5th vow kiska addition tha?" → Mahavira
⚠ TRAP 03 — Digambara vs Shwetambara
Shwetambara = White clothes · Women CAN attain Moksha · North India
Digambara = No clothes (Sky-clad) · South India (Karnataka)
Chandragupta Maurya → Digambara (went with Bhadrabahu to South)
Sthulabhadra → Shwetambara (stayed in North)
⚠ TRAP 04 — Bindusara vs Chandragupta
Chandragupta Maurya = Jain (most prominent Jain emperor, Sallekhana)
Bindusara = Ajivika sect (NOT Jain!)
Ye ek common confusion hai — dono Maurya rulers hain!
⚠ TRAP 05 — Council Locations
1st Jain Council = Pataliputra (Bihar) · 300 BCE · Sthulabhadra
2nd Jain Council = Vallabhi (Gujarat) · 512 CE · Devardhi Kshamashraman
Buddhist vs Jain council locations confuse karte hain — Vallabhi = JAIN (Gujarat)
⚠ TRAP 06 — Mahavira's Key Places
Born: Kundagram, Vaishali (Bihar)
Enlightenment: Jrimbhikagrama (Rijupalika river bank)
Nirvana: Pavapuri, near Rajgir (Bihar)
Teen alag jagah hain — teen alag questions aate hain!

Bimbisara = Called "Shrenika" in Jain texts
He was contemporary of Mahavira.
"Shrenika" puchha jaaye to answer = Bimbisara
