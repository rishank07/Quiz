💡 Core Beliefs ▼
  • Non-Theistic: No belief in God or Soul (Anatta)
  • Karma & Rebirth: Strong emphasis on both
  • Vedas: Rejected the authority of Vedas
  • Goal: Nirvana (Liberation)
  • Values: Truth (Satya) + Non-Violence (Ahimsa)

🔀 Division of Buddhism — 3 Sects ▼
🚌 Hinayana (Lesser Vehicle)
Belief: No Idol Worship
Faith: Strict original teachings
Language: Pali
Goal: Individual salvation (Arhat)
🚀 Mahayana (Greater Vehicle)
Belief: Idol Worship (Buddha as God)
Faith: Bodhisattvas
Language: Sanskrit
Goal: Salvation for all
⚡ Vajrayana (Diamond Vehicle)
Basis: Tantric philosophy (Magic/Spells)
Emerged: Medieval period
📚 Tripitaka — Buddhist Literature Language: Pali ▼
Text Meaning Content
Sutta Pitaka Basket of Discourse Core teachings & dialogues of Buddha · Largest Pitaka
Vinaya Pitaka Basket of Discipline Rules and conduct for monks and nuns
Abhidhamma Pitaka Basket of Philosophy Metaphysics and philosophy
  • Dipavamsa & Mahavamsa: Sri Lankan chronicles discussing Buddhist history
  • First written down: 1st Century BCE in Sri Lanka under King Vattagamani

⚠ TRAP 01 — Council Locations (Buddhist vs Jain)
Buddhist 1st = Rajagriha · Buddhist 2nd = Vaishali · Buddhist 3rd = Pataliputra · Buddhist 4th = Kashmir
Jain 1st = Pataliputra · Jain 2nd = Vallabhi
Mnemonic: Buddhist = Ra-Va-Pa-Ku | Rulers = Aj-Ka-A-K
⚠ TRAP 02 — Pitaka Compiled at Which Council
1st Council: Sutta Pitaka + Vinaya Pitaka
3rd Council (Ashoka): Abhidhamma Pitaka compiled
2nd Council: No new text — Sangha split only
⚠ TRAP 03 — Hinayana vs Mahayana Language
Hinayana = Pali (strict, original teachings, individual salvation)
Mahayana = Sanskrit (idol worship, Buddha as God, salvation for all)
Vajrayana = Tantric (medieval period)
⚠ TRAP 04 — Largest Stupa vs Largest Buddhist Temple
Largest Stupa in world = Kesariya (Bihar)
Largest Buddhist temple = Borobudur (Indonesia) — Shailendra dynasty
Oldest Stupa = Piprahwa (UP) — Kapilavastu relics
⚠ TRAP 05 — First Bhikkhuni
First Bhikkhuni = Prajapati Gautami (Buddha's stepmother — who raised him)
Uski wajah se hi Buddha ke Sangha mein women ko allow kiya gaya.
⚠ TRAP 06 — Buddha's Life Key Ages
Left home at age 29 (Four Sights seen at Kapilavastu)
Enlightenment at age 35 (Bodh Gaya, Vaishakh Purnima)
Death at 483 BCE (Kushinagar) — born 563 BCE → lived 80 years
⚠ TRAP 07 — Nalanda University Founder
Nalanda = Kumaragupta I (Gupta dynasty)
Vikramashila = Dharmapala (Pala dynasty)
Odantapuri = Gopala (Pala dynasty)
Nalanda ko Gupta ne banaya — Pala ne nahi!
⚠ TRAP 08 — Milinda Panha
Milinda Panha = Dialogue between King Menander I (Milinda) and Monk Nagasena
Language = Pali · Meaning = "Questions of Milinda"
Menander = Indo-Greek ruler — NOT Indian ruler!
