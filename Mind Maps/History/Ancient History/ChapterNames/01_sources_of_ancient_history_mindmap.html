⚠ TRAP 01 — Coins
Indo-Greek = First Gold Coins (Pehle gold coins)
Kushans = Purest Gold Coins (Sabse pure sone ke sikke)
Guptas = Most Gold Coins issued (Sabse zyada gold coins)
Exam me teeno alag-alag puchhe jaate hain — confuse mat ho!
⚠ TRAP 02 — Caves
Ashoka → Barabar Hills (4 caves — Sudama, Karna Chaupad, Vishva Jhopdi, Lomas Rishi)
Dasharatha → Nagarjuni Hills (Gopi, Vedika, Vapiya)
Dono ne Ajivikas ko caves gift ki thi — ye important hai!
⚠ TRAP 03 — Firsts
First Sati evidence: Eran Inscription (King Bhanugupta)
First Huna invasion record: Bhitari Pillar (Skandagupta)
First Sanskrit grammar book: Ashtadhyayi (Panini)
First political book: Arthashastra (Chanakya)
⚠ TRAP 04 — Stay Duration (Chinese Travelers)
Faxian = 14 years (Chandragupta II ka time, 402 CE)
Xuanzang = 15 years (Harsha ka time, 630 CE)
Sung Yun = 3 years (518 CE)
"14 ya 15" — exam me yahi confuse karta hai!
⚠ TRAP 05 — Arabic Travelers
Al-Biruni: Came with Mahmud Ghazni (1017 CE), wrote Kitab-ul-Hind
Ibn Battuta: Came 1333 CE, wrote Rihla, appointed Qazi by Muhammad bin Tughlaq
Dono Arabic hain but alag-alag rulers ke time aaye!
⚠ TRAP 06 — Boghazkoi Inscription
Boghazkoi (1400 BCE) mentions Indra, Mitra, Varuna, Nasatya — ye Vedic gods hain.
This is evidence of Aryan migration from Iran eastward — aur ye Iran mein mila tha, India mein nahi!
⚠ TRAP 07 — Vedas
Rigveda = Hymns collection (Mantron ka sangrah)
Samaveda = Musical hymns + First music reference — ye confuse karta hai
Atharvaveda = Charms & Spells (Jaadu-Tona) — alag hai baaki se
Yajurveda = Rituals & Sacrifices
