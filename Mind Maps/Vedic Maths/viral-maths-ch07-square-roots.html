Square Root of Any Number — Guess Approach
Example: √7921
Step 1: Find nearby squares: 85² = 7225 Step 2: Next: 90² = 8100 Step 3: 7921 lies between 7225 and 8100, so root lies between 85 and 90 Step 4: Units digit of 7921 is 1 → the root must end in a digit that gives 1 when squared Step 5: Only 9² ends in 1 → Answer = 89
⚡ QUICK RECALL — Units Digit Mapping
Square ends in 1 → root ends in 1 or 9 Square ends in 4 → root ends in 2 or 8 Square ends in 9 → root ends in 3 or 7 Square ends in 6 → root ends in 4 or 6 Square ends in 5 → root ends in 5 Square ends in 0 → root ends in 0
⚠ EXAM TRAP
Ek units digit se do possible answers aa sakte hain (jaise 1 → 1 ya 9) — tabhi Step 1-3 ka range-bounding zaroori hai taaki sahi wala choose kar sako.
Square Root of Number Ending with 25 — Guess Approach
Example: √5625
Step 1: Break the number leaving 2 digits from the right: 56 | 25 Step 2: Square root of 25 (fixed) = 5 Step 3: Find factors of 56 whose difference is 1: 7×8 = 56 Step 4: Take the SMALLER factor: 7 Step 5: Combine: root = 75
⚡ QUICK RECALL
Numbers ending in 25 ka square root hamesha khud bhi 5 pe end hota hai. Left part ko do consecutive numbers ke product ke roop mein todo, smaller factor lo.
⚠ EXAM TRAP
Hamesha SMALLER factor lena hai, bigger nahi — 56=7×8 mein 7 sahi answer deta hai (75²=5625 ✓), 8 nahi.
