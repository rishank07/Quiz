📌 What This Chapter Covers
  • Approximation = converting a decimal number to a complete (whole) number, but not exactly equal — round off karna.
  • 4 cases hain rounding ke — digit ki value ke hisaab se decide karo kya karna hai.
  • Ek separate technique bhi hai: root approximation — nearby perfect square/cube dhundh ke root nikalna.

Case 1 — Digit After Decimal is MORE than 5
Example: 54.8
Digit right of decimal (8) > 5 Add 1 to the digit left of decimal: 54 → 55 Answer: 55
Case 2 — Digit After Decimal is LESS than 5
Example: 54.3
Digit right of decimal (3) < 5 No change to the left-side digit Answer: 54
Case 3 — Digit After Decimal is EXACTLY 5
Example: 54.5
Digit right of decimal is exactly 5 No change to the original number Answer: 54.5 (stays as-is)
⚠ EXAM TRAP
Case 3 mein number ko round UP ya DOWN nahi karte — number bilkul same rehta hai jaisa tha (54.5 hi rehta hai, na 54 na 55). Yeh common misconception hai ki exactly-5 hamesha round up hota hai — is book ke rule mein aisa nahi hai.
Case 4 — Digit is 5, AND Next Digit is MORE than 5
Example: 34.58
Step 1: Digit right of decimal is 5, next digit (8) > 5 Step 2: Since next digit > 5, treat the 5 as incremented to 6 Step 3: Now apply Case 1 logic (6 > 5): add 1 to left digit 34 → 35 Answer: 35
⚡ QUICK RECALL — All 4 Cases At a Glance
Digit >5 → round UP | Digit <5 → round DOWN | Digit =5 exactly (nothing after) → NO CHANGE | Digit=5 but next digit>5 → treat as 6, then round UP
Root Approximation — Nearest Perfect Square/Cube
Find the nearby value which IS a perfect square or perfect cube, then compute its root
Example: √37
Nearby perfect square: 36 (very close to 37) √37 ≈ √36 = 6
Example: √126
Nearby perfect square: 125 is a perfect CUBE (5³), not square Actually nearby perfect square: 121 (11²) √126 ≈ √121 = 11
⚡ QUICK RECALL
Yeh technique tabhi kaam karti hai jab number kisi perfect square/cube ke BAHUT paas ho — exam mein aise hi questions banaye jaate hain taaki approximation clean nikle.
⚠ EXAM TRAP
Squares 1–100 aur cubes 1–25 (Ch01 aur Ch08 se) fluent yaad hone chahiye — warna "nearby perfect square/cube" dhundhna slow ho jaayega.
