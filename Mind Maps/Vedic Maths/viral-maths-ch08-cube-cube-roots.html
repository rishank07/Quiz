Cube Root Right Approach (Guess Method)
Example: ∛12167
Step 1: Break the number leaving 3 digits from the right: 12 | 167 Step 2: Cube of which number is closest to (below) 12? → 2³=8, so tens digit = 2 Step 3: Units digit of 12167 is 7 → which digit's cube ends in 7? → 3³=27, ends in 7 → units digit = 3 Step 4: Combine: 23
⚡ QUICK RECALL — Cube Units-Digit Mapping
Cube ends in: 1 → root ends in 1 Cube ends in: 2 → root ends in 8 Cube ends in: 3 → root ends in 7 Cube ends in: 4 → root ends in 4 Cube ends in: 5 → root ends in 5 Cube ends in: 6 → root ends in 6 Cube ends in: 7 → root ends in 3 Cube ends in: 8 → root ends in 2 Cube ends in: 9 → root ends in 9 Cube ends in: 0 → root ends in 0
⚠ EXAM TRAP
Sirf 2 aur 8, aur 3 aur 7 flip hote hain — baaki digits (1,4,5,6,9,0) apne aap same rehte hain. Yeh cube-root mapping square-root mapping se DIFFERENT hai (square roots mein 1↔9 aur 4↔8 tha) — dono ko mix mat karo.
