i. Human body → ?

iii. Mouth when tooth decay starts → ?

i. मानव शरीर → ?
ii. अम्लीय वर्षा → ?

  • pH of acid rain drops below 5.6 (~4.2–4.4), mostly due to oxides of sulphur and nitrogen reacting with water vapour.
