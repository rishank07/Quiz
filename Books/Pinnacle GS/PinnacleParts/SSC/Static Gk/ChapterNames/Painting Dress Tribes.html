Santhal community is an ethnic group of ________ India.

✅ Correct Answer: (A)
  • Eastern.
  • Santhal community is primarily found in the states of Jharkhand, West Bengal, Odisha, and Bihar.
  • Tribes in five distinct regions are : Himalayan region (Gaddi, the Jaunsari, the Naga, etc.), Middle India (Munda, the Santhal), Western India (Bhil, the Grasia), South Indian Region (Toda, the Chenchu) and the Islands Region (with tribes like the Onge in Bay of Bengal).
  • Highest Scheduled Tribes Populated state - Madhya Pradesh.
  • पूर्वी।
  • संथाल समुदाय मुख्य रूप से झारखंड, पश्चिम बंगाल, ओडिशा और बिहार राज्यों में पाया जाता है।

  • सर्वाधिक अनुसूचित जनजाति जनसंख्या वाला राज्य - मध्य प्रदेश।
