The consent of .......... required for alternating its boundaries according to the Constitutional provisions.

  • Article 3 authorises the parliament to form new states or alter the areas, boundaries, names of the existing states without the state's consent.

Which one of the following pairs is not correctly matched?
निम्नलिखित में से कौन सा युग्म सही ढंग से सुमेलित नहीं है?
✅ Correct Answer: (D)
  • According to the Article 3 of the Constitution, Parliament may by law form new States, and alter the area, boundaries or the name of any existing State.
  • With respect to the alteration of area, boundary or name of a state, it is expected, from the concerned State Legislature to express its views on the issue, under the provision of the Article 3. But the views expressed by the state legislature are not binding on the parliament.
