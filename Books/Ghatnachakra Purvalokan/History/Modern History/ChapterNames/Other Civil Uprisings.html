Arrange the following uprisings against the British colonial power in India prior to 1857 in the correct order:

(ii) Kutch Rebellion (कच्छ विद्रोह)
(iii) Sepoy mutiny at Vellore (वेल्लोर में सिपाही विद्रोह)
